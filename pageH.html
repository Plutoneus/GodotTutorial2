H - Adding a Camera

  • Now, you will be adding a camera to the scene so that you can see what's happening when you run the program.

  • 1. Add a child to your World node, search and select "Camera."

  • 2. With the Camera selected, Click and drag the blue arrow towards the direction it's pointing.

  • 3. With the Camera selected, Click and drag the green arrow towards the direction it's pointing.

  • 4. You may adjust the Camera's position with the methods shown in steps 2 and 3 until it's around the position in the image above.

  • 5. Press the Play button in the top right of your screen to run the program.

  • 6. Your program should look similar to the gif above. You are now finished with the tutorial.

    Congratulations on creating your first 3D Godot scene!!!

Footnote I: To recreate the gif from page B, you can duplicate your Box nodes (RigidBodies) by right clicking them and selecting Duplicate. Rearrange them however you'd like with the movement options shown on this page and page G
