F - Proper Flooring

  • Congratulations! You have created your first fully-functioning physics node. Your Scene tab should look identical to mine in the image above.

  • 1. Be sure to have the Floor selected in the Scene tab. Click on the Transform parameter in the Inspector to show more parameters. You modified the Floor's children in the previous steps, but now you will modify the Floor itself.

  • 2. Click on the numbers under the Scale parameter and change the X and Z by typing numbers. I chose for my floor to be 4 x 1 x 4.
