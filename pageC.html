C - Saving and Running a Blank Scene

  • (OPTIONAL) 0. After hitting ctrl+S, this menu will pop up. Click Create Folder to create a new folder within our project. I will be naming this folder "Scenes", as it will only contain a single scene in the scope of this tutorial.

  • 1. Click Save at the bottom of this window to save your current scene as a file within the project. From now on, you may push ctrl+S to save your scene at any point.

  • 2. After returning to your main project screen, either push F5 on your keyboard or click the play button at the top right of your screen.

  • 3. After returning to your main project screen, either push F5 on your keyboard or click the play button at the top right of your screen. If you see a gray screen like mine above, perfect! This is your blank canvas that will later contain visible nodes.

Footnote I: I am on a windows machine, and will be saying "ctrl" which always translates to "cmd" for mac users.
